Casual day-to-day makeup

Today I am going to show you a fairly casual and quick makeup that you can use in your day to day life that does not require many implements or time.

what will we need

  • Serum
  • Concealer
  • Blush
  • Eyeliner
  • Gloss
  • Blending brush

Step 1

Apply serum all over the face and distribute evenly with your fingers in circles, wait for it to dry.

step 2

Apply concealer to dark circles and eye lines, and blend gently and evenly with your fingers

Step 3

With the blending brush, apply blush in the tear or giving the shape you want to have

Step 4

Delineate to your liking, depending on the shape of your eye or how you feel more comfortable

Step 5

Apply gloss or lipstick, depending on your preference
